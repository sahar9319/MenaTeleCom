## Beyond the "Dumb Pipe": How MENA Telcos are Funding the Future with AI

The traditional telecom model is dead. In the MENA region, top operators are executing a brilliant decoupling strategy: using AI to automate the network, slashing OpEx, and recycling that capital directly into high-margin enterprise tech, sovereign cloud, and fintech.

According to GSMA Intelligence, 91% of regional AI adoption focuses on Operational Optimization. The playbook? Optimize the baseline to fund the future.

Here is how four major giants shifted their portfolios in FY2024:

### stc | The Cloud Pivot

- The Shift: Digital services now drive 25% of total revenue, led by its enterprise arm, *solutions by stc*.
- The AI Engine: Ran Nokia's MantaRay to execute 10,000 autonomous network operations per hour—boosting throughput by 10% and delaying costly hardware upgrades.
- The Win: Recycled those savings straight into expanding its cloud footprint for lucrative Saudi Vision 2030 contracts.

### e& Group | The Techco Transformation

- The Shift: Enterprise digital solutions are growing at twice the pace of traditional connectivity.
- The AI Engine: Operationalized 1,100+ AI use cases. Real-time video compression eliminated premature fiber CapEx, while AI automated customer care to cut OpEx by 25%.
- The Win: Subsidized the aggressive expansion of *e& enterprise*, shifting the group from a regional telco to a global tech conglomerate.

### Ooredoo Group | The Sovereign AI Cloud

- The Shift: B2B verticals—Data Centers, Fintech, and Cybersecurity—are growing at a high-teens annual rate.
- The AI Engine: Deployed Ericsson's AI Energy Saver to put radio networks into micro-sleep during low traffic, cutting radio power consumption by 15%.
- The Win: Lowered the cost-per-gigabyte to free up balance sheet capacity for Sovereign AI Clouds, securing highly regulated national data contracts.

### 🇰🇼 Zain Group | The Autonomous Network

- The Shift: *ZainTECH* revenue more than doubled year-over-year, while fintech grew at a high double-digit rate.
- The AI Engine: Implemented a Level 4 Autonomous Network with Huawei, using zero-human-intervention self-healing to turn the physical network into an invisible, low-cost utility.
- The Win: Shifted human capital to software. Result? In-house fintech algorithms cut micro-loan defaults by double digits.

MENA is now a global testing ground for 5G-Advanced. Operators aren't disappearing—they are decoupling. By using AI to commoditize their own infrastructure, they are freeing up the cash to dominate digital banking, cloud orchestration, and enterprise software.
