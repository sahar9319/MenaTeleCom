### **Why Capital is Moving Behind the Dashboard**

- **Compounding Efficiency:** Traditional operations scale linearly (more nodes = more engineers). Autonomous OSS decouples this, allowing capacity to scale exponentially while flattening OpEx.

- **Predictive Mitigation:** Next-gen platforms resolve network degradation by **30–40% before it impacts the end-user**, shifting operators from reactive troubleshooting to proactive anomaly management.

- **Dynamic Power Savings:** AI models dynamically put cells into micro-sleep modes based on real-time traffic density, driving double-digit drops in heavy RAN energy costs.
